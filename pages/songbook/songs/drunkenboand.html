Drunken BoAndShanty

What shall we do with the drunken BoAnd, what shall we do with the drunken BoAnd, what shall we do with the drunken BoAnd, earl-aye in the morning? Hooray and up she rises, hooray and up she rises, hooray and up she rises, earl-ay in the morning.

Put him in the long-boat until he’s sober…

Pull out the plug and wet him all over…

Beat him with a cat ’til his back is bleedin’…

Put him in the scuppers with a hose-pipe on him…

Heave him by the leg in a running bowlin’…
